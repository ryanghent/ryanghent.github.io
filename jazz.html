Jazz.

    I love jazz. I have been playing piano for about 10 years, and sax for 5. I started playing classical music, and attained distinction in Grade 8 sax. Then I discovered jazz, which is far more expressive.

    I have played with the National Youth Jazz Orchestra, the award winning saxophonist Phil Meadows, and now I play in jazz jams in Oxford. Here is a photo from Jazz Soc at the Mad Hatter, playing with Garfunkel, of which I hope to be a member next year.
