Jazz.

    I love jazz.

    I play alto sax, and piano. I have played with the National Youth Jazz Orchestra, the award winning saxophonist Phil Meadows, and various bands at university, including: a 20-piece big band, a soul band, and a New-Orleans style cover band. I've played live gigs to an audience of over a thousand people. I am a regular at jazz jams.

    More recently, I've been in a reggae/ska band, and have started playing with DJs.
