Climbing.

    This year, I have managed to do some climbing. In the first few weeks of term, I slipped away to Simmonds Yat, in the Wye Valley and did some trad climbing. We climbed this gorgeous V Diff called Vertigo, which was up the front face of this outcrop, and then we also climbed it from the other side.

    I also secured my place on the climbing team. While we haven’t met in person yet, the online workouts have kept us all fit over the second lockdown, and I can’t wait to get back climbing again! I am also a qualified climbing instructor.
