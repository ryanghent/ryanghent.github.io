Climbing.

    In 2024 and 2025, I was lucky to be in North Wales, with some of the best climbing in the UK, and scrambling in the world. Here, I'm seconding the E1 'Cemetery Gates' in the Llanberis pass. I managed to get out regularly, doing lots of trad, as well as some of the fantastic hiking and scrambling.

    In the past, I have mostly climbed sport, on seacliffs around the UK, in Tenerife, and on a trip I organised to Kalymnos, Greece.

    In London, I boulder or lead regularly. I am also a qualified Climbing Wall Instructor.
